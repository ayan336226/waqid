Video Editing Service Policy

We respect the confidentiality of all media files and project details shared with us. This policy outlines how we handle, process, and protect your video content during and after the editing process.

Media We Receive

We may receive raw footage, audio files, scripts, graphics, and other creative assets necessary to complete your project. These files are stored securely and used solely for the purpose of editing and producing your content.

How We Use Your Media

Your media and project details are used exclusively to deliver the agreed editing services, including but not limited to trimming, color grading, sound enhancement, motion graphics, and final export in the required formats.

Confidentiality

We do not share or distribute your raw files, completed videos, or any project information without your explicit consent. Any public sharing will only occur after written approval from you.

Client Rights

All final edited videos delivered to you are for your personal or commercial use as agreed upon. Unless specified in a contract, the editor retains the right to showcase completed work in a private portfolio or demo reel.

File Retention

Raw project files may be stored for up to 30 days after delivery unless otherwise requested. After this period, they may be deleted to free storage space.

Data Security

All files are handled with professional care, stored on secure devices, and protected from unauthorized access or distribution.

Contact

If you have questions regarding this policy or require changes to our standard practices, please contact us.
